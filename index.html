Our Promise

✦

What sets our kitchen apart — and keeps guests coming back for decades

Made from scratch,
every morning

Starting at dawn, our chefs prepare numerous batters from authentic, coveted recipes — never from packaged mixes. All carefully blended by hand using fresh ingredients of the highest quality.

Our sourdough starter has been handed down from generation to generation, and is what gives our pancakes their incomparable light, fine texture.

✦

Locally sourced,
with love

Our orange juice, grapefruit juice, and lemonade are freshly squeezed every morning. Our butter exceeds USDA 93 score — the finest available, rendered in-house.

We use locally roasted coffee, locally sourced ingredients wherever possible, and everything we can make in-house, we do. Nothing can replace fresh products for food value and taste.

Our Story

· · ✦ · ·

The Birch Door Café brings to the table three generations of restaurant excellence. It all began with my grandparents' restaurant in Chicago over 50 years ago. Following in their footsteps, my parents opened the Oak Table Café in Sequim in 1981.

Growing up in my parents' restaurant alongside my two siblings, we fell in love with the family business. After graduating from Western Washington University in 2008, I helped my sister open her restaurant in Silverdale, then in 2011, along with my wife Taria, we opened The Maple Counter Café in Walla Walla.

Carrying the flag of our family's tradition of warm hospitality and delicious food, Taria and I are thrilled to plant our roots here in Bellingham. We hope that through our handcrafted meals and caring service we can be a bright spot in your day.
